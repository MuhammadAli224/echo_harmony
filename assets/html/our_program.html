Our Vision

We aim to create a work environment full of positivity, creativity, and balance between work and fun — because we believe happiness drives productivity and innovation.

1. Strengthening Social Activities

  • Monthly team talks with small symbolic rewards for presenters 🎤
  • Board games like chess, domino, and group challenges 🎲
  • Special welcome kits for new employees 🎁
  • Weekly or monthly English Day activities 🗣️

2. Enhancing the Work Environment

  • Encourage and reward employees who improve internal projects 💡
  • Introduce an Achievements Board to honor outstanding employees 🏆
  • Use in-house talents to improve workplace tools and culture 🤝

3. Building a Flexible Work Atmosphere

  • Plan events with flexible timing to suit everyone’s schedules 🕒
  • Ensure activities are sustainable and easy to continue 🌱
  • Consider married employees’ timing and family life ❤️
  • Celebrate all occasions and holidays 🎉

4. Boosting Happiness and Wellbeing

  • Launch a reward program for the most active participants 🥇
  • Hold fun contests inside and outside the office 🎯
  • Celebrate monthly birthdays with the team 🎂
  • Annual “Dream Day” team outing 🌄

5. The Happiness Fund 💰

Introducing a special fund to support social activities and reduce employee expenses. Each member contributes a small amount — just 2 JOD per month — and the fund will be supported in multiple creative ways throughout the year.

Our Promise

We will work tirelessly to make our workplace more vibrant, creative, and full of harmony. Entertainment is not a waste of time — it’s an investment in wellbeing and motivation.

Your vote is a vote for fun, creativity, and collaboration! 🎉
